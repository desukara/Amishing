Welcome to Amishing.com

Many people have questions about the Amish, but much of what they hear is incomplete, romanticized, exaggerated, or misunderstood.

This site exists to provide clear and respectful explanations about Amish beliefs, daily life, church, family, technology, clothing, work, education, history, and traditions.

The goal is not to criticize the Amish, mock them, turn them into a tourist attraction, or encourage outsiders to join them. The goal is understanding.
